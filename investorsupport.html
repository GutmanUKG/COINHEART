Investor Support Associate

at Blockchain First (View all jobs)
Remote
Apply

Blockchain First is where the best crypto projects in the world raise capital and grow their communities. Through our token sale platform, we've helped projects like Filecoin, Blockstack, and Algorand raise over $500 million. Through our community-building tools, we've helped projects like DFINITY, 0x, and Dapper Labs (makers of CryptoKitties) engage developers and crypto enthusiasts. We are backed by top-tier investors, have offices in the United Kingdom, and are just getting started in NYC

The investor support team at Blockchain First is the primary interface between Blockchain First and the investors using our suite of products. Developing and maintaining trust with users is a core part of our business. You’ll answer inbound questions about our product and identify and communicate issues in concise language.

Blockchain First is planning to introduce wallets and trading functionality over the next few months. This role will initially be focused on building operational processes for these products. 

We’re especially interested in candidates who have held support or operational roles. This is a remote, full-time position. We strongly prefer that candidates be able to work on a US time-zone (e.g. ET/PT).

Who you are
  • You’re excited about cryptocurrency. Coins-Bay’s mission is to help the best crypto companies succeed by serving as a trusted advisor in the space. We’re incredibly excited by the power of distributed ledger technology, and you should be too.
  • You’re entrepreneurial. This role is autonomous - you’re really excited about proposing and testing ideas based on issues and opportunities you see.
  • You’re an excellent communicator. You’ll be the primary interface between Blockchain First and our users and be responsible for diagnosing user issues and helping users navigate our products.

What you will do

  • You’ll contribute in all areas. You will contact potential investors about particular alfa ico projects that aren’t listed yet via communication means (e-mail, phone)
  • You’ll Write proposal letters. based on ico whitepapers .
  • You’ll rapidly grow and improve. At Blockchain First you’ll be working with some of the smartest engineers you’ve ever met, who love to learn and teach each other. You’ll find yourself stretching to new areas.
  • You’ll Identify client needs and suggest appropriate products. Proactively seek new growth opportunities, primarily through networking, prospecting referrals, seminars, etc
  • You’ll learn about startups. Achieve and surpass the target as set for pre-approved available product offering
  • You’ll be an independant contractor. Understand and apply responsibilities and activities in accordance with "Know Your Clients" guidelines
  • You’ll create the future. Crypto is a far bigger deal than most people realize, and at Blockchain First you will be be at the forefront of it. There are all sorts of technical challenges you’ll be working though and new questions you’ll have to answer, in partnership with founders for the leading crypto projects across the globe. If you’re successful, you’ll build something the world has never before seen.
